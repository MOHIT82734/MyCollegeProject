Campus Clubs & Societies

Explore our vibrant student communities

Swargini

Swaragini is the art and cultural society that brings out the artist in you. From dance to drama, this club celebrates every form of artistic expression.

Kavyanjali

Kavyanjali is a platform for poetry and literature enthusiasts to share, perform and appreciate the richness of words and emotions.

Debate Society

The Debate Society sharpens your intellect and reasoning. Join thought-provoking discussions and enhance your public speaking skills.

NSS (National Service Scheme)

NSS empowers students to take part in social service and community development. It's about making a positive impact in society.

Rang-MUNCH

NCC instills discipline, patriotism and leadership in students through military-based training and activities.
